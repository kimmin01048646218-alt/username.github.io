Uniwing

Admissions mentoring platform connecting high school students with university mentors.

Overview

Uniwing operates a Naver Café called “Yeonhapbu – Yonsei University Admission Charm”, and currently provides a mentoring service that connects high school students aspiring to enter Yonsei University with enrolled Yonsei students. The platform offers school record mentoring and study routine mentoring, and has gathered 73 café members so far.

• https://cafe.naver.com/uniwing123
